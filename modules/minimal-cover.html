Equivalent FD Sets & Minimal Cover

Two FD sets are equivalent when each implies the other вЂ” they constrain the data identically. Among all equivalent sets, the minimal (canonical) cover is the leanest: every right side is a single attribute, no left side carries an extraneous attribute, and no FD is redundant. It's the clean starting point for normalization.
